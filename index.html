Getting started:
  • Programs are initially unchecked — click programs in the legend to show on the map
  • Use the Program, Parameter, and EOV dropdowns to filter what's displayed
  • Click Apply after changing filters, then Zoom to fit the results in view
  • Click any hex cell on the map to see details about that monitoring program
  • Sample Locations — the number of distinct lat/lon points recorded inside that hex cell
  • Toggle Dischargers, Transects, and Bathymetry checkboxes in the toolbar for additional layers
🏭 Dischargers
Wastewater and industrial discharge facilities; filter by facility name or parameter
🚢 Transects
Ship survey track lines for tow, trawl, and underway sampling programs
🌊 Bathymetry
GEBCO 2025 seafloor depth — shows shelf, mesopelagic, and deep ocean zones
